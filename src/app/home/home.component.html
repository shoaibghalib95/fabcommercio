F.A.B COMMERCIO is a modern trading and indenting company which is sourcing other companies by offering its services to Project Management companies, Construction companies, and other allied Industries for procuring RO plants, Construction material, Surgical items & other items from world renowned and trusted Manufacturer with international standing. We protect interests of both parties equally and mutually without any discrimination. Our approach towards trading is not just for one time transaction, instead we believe into building long term relationship with local customers and International supplier on the basis of trust, loyalty and mutual interest.
